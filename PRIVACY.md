# Privacy Policy for TLDR - AI Reading Assistant

**Last updated: June 9, 2026**

## Overview

TLDR - AI Reading Assistant ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our browser extension.

## Data We Collect

### Website Content
The extension reads the text content of web pages you visit **only when you actively trigger the extension** (e.g., clicking the extension icon or using the right-click context menu). This content is sent to your configured AI API provider solely for the purpose of generating summaries and answering your questions.

### Data We Do NOT Collect
We do **not** collect, store, or transmit:
- Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
- Financial or payment information
- Authentication credentials
- Personal communications
- Location data
- Browsing history (beyond the active tab when triggered)
- User activity tracking (clicks, mouse position, keystrokes)

## How Data Is Used

- **Page content**: Sent to your own configured AI API (e.g., OpenAI, Google Gemini) for summarization and Q&A. We do not intercept or store this data on our servers.
- **API keys**: Stored locally in your browser using the `chrome.storage` API. Never transmitted to any server other than the AI API endpoint you configure.
- **Preferences and conversation history**: Stored locally in your browser. Never transmitted externally.

## Third-Party Services

The extension allows you to configure your own AI API provider (such as OpenAI or Google Gemini). When you use these services:
- Your API key and page content are sent directly from your browser to the API provider's servers.
- Each provider has their own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.
- We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party services.

## Data Storage

All user data (API keys, preferences, conversation history) is stored locally on your device using browser storage APIs. We do not maintain any external servers or databases.

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell user data to third parties
- Transfer user data for purposes unrelated to the extension's core functionality
- Use user data for creditworthiness or lending purposes

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
